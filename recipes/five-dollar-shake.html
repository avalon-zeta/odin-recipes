$5 Shake

Description

The $5 Shake from Pulp Fiction (1994) that makes you wonder about its price tag.

On a side note, nobody likes to hear "adjusted for inflation" but here you go.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana (peeled)
  • 1 bourbon vanilla pod or half a teaspoon of vanilla paste
  • 300gm clotted cream ice cream
  • 200gm natural yoghurt
  • 250 ml whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons of clear honey
  • 5 cubes of ice
  • Pinch of salt
  • Griottine cherries
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Halve the vanilla pod and scrape the seeds with a small knife.
  2. Put this into a blender with all of the other ingredients.
  3. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  4. Pour into two suitable sized glasses and garnish with the whipped cream and the cherries.
